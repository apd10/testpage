Are exponential improvements in efficiency of AI possible?

Are we using our resources intelligently? Do we really need hundreds of trillions of parameters and Quadrillions of flops ?

Hello, I am Aditya Desai

I am a researcher concerned about the sustainability, affordability and accessibility of Artificial Intelligence (AI). I evaluate the resource (i.e., memory, compute, energy, etc) and utility (i.e., model quality metrics) tradeoffs in AI to reduce resource usage and find representations and schemes that provide better resource-utility tradeoffs. In this endeavor, I use probabilistic algorithms and data-structures to offer solutions to new-age scalability challenges of AI
